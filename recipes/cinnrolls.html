Cinnamon Rolls

The best cinnamon rolls. Can be proofed in the fridge overnight, frozen after baking, or eaten fresh.

Ingredients

Dough:

  • 3/4 cup warm milk (110 F)
  • 7 g quick rise or active yeast
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 egg plus 1 egg yolk
  • 1/4 cup melted butter
  • 3 cups bread flour
  • 3/4 teaspoon salt

Filling:

  • 2/3 cup brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons cinnamon
  • 1/4 cup softened butter

Frosting:

  • 4 oz cream cheese
  • 3 tablespoons softened butter
  • 3/4 cup powdered sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract

Directions

  1. Put warm milk in electric mixer with yeast, egg, yolk and butter.
  2. Stir in flour and salt and knead with dough hook for 8 minutes.
  3. Rise for 1-1.5 hours until doubled in size.
  4. Roll out to 14x9 in, spread butter, brown sugar and cinnamon on top.
  5. Roll dough from 9 in side, cut into 9 pieces.
  6. Rise for 30-45.
  7. Bake at 350 for 20-25 minutes until golden brown.
  8. Mix frosting and spread after cooling for 10 minutes.
