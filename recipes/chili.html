Chili By George

This is the best chili recipe I've found. It's simple, but you can add mushrooms, corn, or anything else you want to it.

Ingredients

  • 2 lb ground beef
  • 1 can tomato juice
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of beans (any kind you want)
  • 1 chopped onion
  • 1/2 chopped green pepper
  • 1/8 teaspoon cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon white sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon cumin
  • 1/4 cup chili powder

Directions

  1. Brown ground beef in pan. Drain and crumble.
  2. Add the beef and remaining ingredients to big pot. Bring to a boil and simmer for 1 to 1.5 hours.
